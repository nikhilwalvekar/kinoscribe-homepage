Presets allows you to use one of our ready made transitions

  • kino-transition-left-to-right-emphasis
  • kino-transition-top-to-bottom-emphasis
  • kino-transition-box
  • kino-transition-3d-spiral
  • kino-transition-file-cabinet
  • ...

Usage

<body>
  <kino-pres
       transition=
         "kino-transition-left-to-right-emphasis">
    <kino-slide >
        <section class="slide">
            Slide 1
        </section>
    </kino-slide>
    .... More such slides
  </kino-pres>
</body>
